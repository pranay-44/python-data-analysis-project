Our Story

A Legacy of Healing Since 1999

Founded in 1999, Global 5 Hospital began as a 150-bed multi-specialty center with a single mission: to provide world-class healthcare that is accessible and compassionate. Over 25 years, we have grown into one of India's most trusted healthcare institutions.

Today, we operate a 600-bed state-of-the-art campus in Mumbai with 40+ departments, 350+ specialist doctors, and a team exceeding 2,000 healthcare professionals dedicated to your well-being.
