Our Services

Comprehensive Patient Care

From admission to recovery, we ensure the highest standard of care at every step of your healthcare journey.

24/7 Emergency & Trauma

Dedicated trauma bays with rapid response teams, equipped for all medical emergencies including cardiac arrest, stroke, polytrauma and acute poisoning.

Critical Care & ICU

Multi-specialty ICUs including Cardiac ICU, Neuro-ICU, Surgical ICU, PICU and NICU with one-to-one nursing and advanced life support.

Advanced Diagnostics

3T MRI, 128-slice CT, PET-CT, digital X-ray, echocardiography, endoscopy and AI-powered pathology for rapid, accurate diagnoses.

Genetic & Molecular Lab

Chromosomal analysis, molecular diagnostics, BRCA gene testing and next-generation sequencing for personalized medicine and cancer profiling.

Rehabilitation Center

Comprehensive physiotherapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy and neurological rehabilitation with dedicated therapists and modern equipment.

International Patient Services

Dedicated team offering visa assistance, airport transfers, translation, accommodation and end-to-end care coordination for medical tourists.

Blood Bank & Transfusion

24/7 blood bank with comprehensive components, leukoreduction filters, autologous blood donation and cell salvage programs.

Telemedicine & e-Consult

Virtual OPD consultations with specialists via video call, digital prescriptions, and follow-up care from the comfort of your home.

Nutrition & Dietetics

Personalized dietary counseling for diabetes, heart disease, obesity, renal disorders and post-surgical nutrition planning.
